Kazi Nazrul Islam
The National Poet of Bangladesh

Robel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of bangladesh.who is espesially recognised as a Bidrohi Kobi(robel Poet) around the Country.Nazrul is the famous poet, mucisian and Philosopher of Bangaldesh. The famous poet nazrul's writing deals with revolution of the period of the british government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of bangladesh. He has rised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around indian sub continent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writing exposed thems such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism including religious cast based and gender based. Throught is career, nazrul's wrote shoet stories, novels and essays but best and best knows for his poems and songs.He Pioneered new music forms such as bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4000 songs(Many recorded on HMV and Gramaphone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti
